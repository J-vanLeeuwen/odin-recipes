It’s the authentic recipe born in the city of Bologna, WITHOUT MOZZARELLA. Oh yes, because, contrary to popular belief, the authentic classic recipe of Lasagna Bolognese does NOT want mozzarella. Lasagna with mozzarella is a VARIANT of this classic Lasagna recipe and you can find it mainly in southern Italy, often together with ricotta. The authentic traditional recipe of lasagna Bolognese is made up of three main preparations: fresh egg pasta (Lasagna Noodles), Bolognese Sauce (Ragu alla Bolognese) and bechamel.
